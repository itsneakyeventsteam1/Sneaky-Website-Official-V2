Registration Process:


- Visit our official tournament website at - https://www.facebook.com/SneakyEventsTeam


Look for the "Tickets" or "Buy Tickets" post form.


Note: You can also directly fill out the form here.



Selecting Tickets:

- Once on the ticketing form, browse through the available ticket options. - Select the type and quantity of tickets you wish to purchase (e.g., General Admission, VIP, etc.).
- Once you finish answering the forms, you will need to message the page to verify your details and also your selected ticket and number of tickets that you will avail.


Checkout Process:

- Once you are verified, an admin will ask you to pay the total amount of the tickets that you wish to purchase.
- Fill in the required information accurately, including your name, and email.
- For people who will buy multiple number of tickets, we will be asking for the names of your friends in order to assign them a specified ticket number.


Payment:

- The payment method will be GCASH only (09951902785 J*****N B.)
- Enter your payment details securely.


Confirmation:

- After completing the payment process, you will receive a confirmation email with your ticket details.
- Save this email for your records. Your ticket(s) will be used for entry to the tournament.


Entry to the Tournament:

- On the day of the tournament, present either a printed copy of your ticket or display the electronic version on your mobile device at the entry gate.


Additional Information:

- For any inquiries or assistance regarding ticket purchases, feel free to contact our customer support team at sneakyeventsteam@gmail.com


Important Note:

- Tickets are non-transferable and should not be resold. Any attempt to resell tickets may result in cancellation of entry without refund.
- Tickets are sold on a first-come, first-served basis, so we encourage you to secure yours early.
- Make sure to read and understand any terms and conditions associated with the ticket purchase, including refund policies.
